FIELD STANDARDS
========================================================================

Mandatory fields for all consultation paradigm tasks:

  id                    - Unique task identifier
  task_type             - core_consultation|structured_query|mcq_screening|specialist_consultation
  consultation_scenario - primary_care|pre_consultation_data|specialist_consultation|differential_diagnosis
  question              - "Patient asks: {original_question}" or "Nurse asks: {question}"
  answer                - "Doctor answers: {answer}"
  domain                - Always "clinical/consultation"
  ticket                - Original question text
  original_format       - Source dataset name

    ↓
    Final diagnosis/advice in natural language
